- The aorta consists of five main anatomic segments: [^ase-aorta]
	- the [[Aortic Root|aortic root]], 
	- the tubular portion of the ascending aorta, 
	- the aortic arch, 
	- the descending thoracic aorta, and 
	- the abdominal aorta
